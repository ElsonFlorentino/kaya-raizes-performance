# Prompts de Imagem IA — Kaya Raízes Performance
## Para uso no ChatGPT (GPT-4o Image Generation)

**Como usar este documento:**
1. Cole o **Bloco de Contexto de Marca** no início de cada conversa nova com o ChatGPT
2. Em seguida, cole o prompt da peça desejada
3. Se quiser gerar várias peças na mesma conversa, mantenha o bloco de contexto ativo e use os prompts individuais um a um

## Bloco de Contexto de Marca
**Cole sempre antes do primeiro prompt de imagem**

```
Brand identity for all visual assets I'll request:

Brand name: Kaya Raízes Performance
Industry: Digital performance marketing for small and medium businesses in Brazil
Positioning: Specialized agency in paid media, measurement and data analysis. The name combines "Raízes" (roots in Portuguese — solid foundations, grounded decisions) with "Performance" (measurable results). The brand promise is: you invest in ads, we make sure you know exactly what is working and why.
Target audience: Pragmatic small business owners and managers who need results, not reports.

Visual identity guidelines:
- Color palette: Deep forest green (#1A3C34), warm amber/gold (#C9882A), off-white (#F5F2EB), dark charcoal (#1C1C1C)
- Style: Modern, clean, professional but approachable. Not cold corporate. Not generic startup.
- Texture direction: Subtle organic textures (paper grain, earth) as accents — not as dominant style
- Typography feel: Bold, direct, sans-serif. Weight and clarity over decoration.
- Photography/illustration style: Real, honest, grounded. No generic stock photos of people pointing at laptops.
- What to avoid: Neon colors, excessive gradients, cartoon style, pie charts with too many colors, clichéd "growth arrow" visuals, stock photo business people.
- Mood reference: Think the visual seriousness of a boutique financial consultancy crossed with the warmth of a local specialist who knows your business by name.
```

## Assets a Gerar

### Asset 1 — Hero Image (Imagem principal, above the fold)

**Dimensões:** 1440x900px (desktop) — gerar também versão 768x900px (mobile crop)
**Posição:** Ocupa metade direita do hero no desktop, fundo no mobile

```
Create a hero illustration for a Brazilian digital performance marketing agency called "Kaya Raízes Performance".

Visual concept: An abstract yet grounded visualization that communicates data-driven decision making. Think: organic root structures that transform into data flow lines, graphs, or network nodes. The roots represent solid foundations; the data lines represent performance and measurability. Not a literal illustration — more of an abstract art piece that suggests meaning.

Style: Minimalist, modern illustration. Deep forest green background (#1A3C34) with warm amber/gold (#C9882A) data elements. Clean off-white (#F5F2EB) accent lines. No text in the image.

Mood: Confident, grounded, intelligent. Not cold. Not generic.

Canvas: 1440x900px, landscape. The left third should be mostly empty/dark to allow overlaid headline text.

Do not include: People, laptops, phones, pie charts, bar charts that look like stock imagery, literal trees or leaves, Brazilian flag elements.
```

**Uso:** Fundo sutil para seções de conteúdo (Problema, Serviços, FAQ)
**Dimensões:** 1440x800px, deve ser tileable

```
Create a subtle background texture for a website section. 

Style: Very subtle paper grain texture in off-white (#F5F2EB) with extremely faint organic micro-patterns — almost invisible, just enough to break pure white. Think premium printed paper or high-end business card stock.

The texture should be nearly seamless so it can tile without visible edges.

No strong patterns, no geometric shapes, no visible design elements. Just a refined, sophisticated background that adds depth without competing with overlaid content.

Canvas: 1440x800px.
```

## Notas Importantes

**Sobre consistência:**
Se o ChatGPT gerar um asset que você gostar muito, salve o prompt exato e adicione: *"This image was generated in our previous session. Maintain exactly the same color palette, stroke weight, illustration style, and overall aesthetic for this new asset."* — Isso ajuda a manter coerência sem precisar recomeçar.

**Sobre o logo:**
Ícones e a OG Image podem ser gerados por IA. O logo final (arquivo SVG limpo para uso em todos os materiais) precisa ser finalizado por um designer ou vetorizado a partir do conceito gerado. IA gera conceito de logo, não arquivo de produção.

**Sobre tamanhos:**
Peça sempre a dimensão exata no prompt. O GPT-4o respeita dimensões quando especificadas. Para o OG Image, 1200x630 é obrigatório — não aceite variação.

**Ordem sugerida de geração:**
1. Asset 1 (Hero) — define o tom visual de tudo
2. Asset 7 (OG Image) — usa o mesmo estilo do Hero
3. Assets 5 e 6 (Ícones) — podem ser gerados juntos
4. Asset 8 (Favicon) — depois de ter o visual geral definido
5. Assets 2, 3 e 9 — backgrounds e texturas por último
